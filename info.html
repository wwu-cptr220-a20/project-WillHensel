Filtering

Filter games based on a variety of attributes including category, name, mechanics, number of players, comments, and rating.

Categorization

Create custom categories to organize your games. You can categorize your games based on what type they are (party, campaign, etc...) or maybe what groups like to play them.

Ratings

Rate the games in your library based on how your group liked it.

Comments

Make custom comments on your games like what which of your groups would be best to play them with. Comments are a great way to add keywords to your games for easy filtering in the future.
